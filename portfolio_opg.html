ABOUT ME

OVERVIEW

As a graphic designer I utilize my creativity, imagination and skill set to transform brands into effective designs. My philosophy is to explore different ways of successfully communicating the mission and identity of the brand, benefiting from the chance to grow from these collaborative experiences. It is from this viewpoint that I make my designs eye-catching and impactful.

EDUCATION

I am a Junior at Portland State University majoring in Graphic Design. Over the years, I have absorbed many skills from professionals in the industry, spanning from a large array of tools for a designer, with an awareness of appropriate designs, and effective communication. Throughout my education, I have collaborated with my peers thus strengthening my ability to work effectively as a team, and learning from the experience and perspective of others.

SKILLS

DESIGN

Deep understanding of the design process for word webs, mind maps, moodboards, concept sketches, art styles, color palettes, type studies, hand lettering, and more.

WEB

Utilizing HTML, CSS, Jquery, Sublime Text, along with constructing wireframes in Adobe XD to create websites.

TYPOGRAPHY

Strong grasp of type treatment including picking appropriate typefaces, and adjusting them to achieve design goals.

ARTISTRY

Knowledgable on design appropriate aesthetics and how to digitally replicate them for design directions.
